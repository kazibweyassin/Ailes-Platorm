# Scholarship Deadlines Verification Guide

**IMPORTANT**: All deadlines in the database should be verified annually with official provider websites. The dates below are based on typical annual cycles and may need adjustment.

## Typical Deadline Patterns by Scholarship

### UK Scholarships
- **Chevening**: Usually opens August, closes early November (typically November 1-2)
- **Gates Cambridge**: Usually closes early October (typically October 1-3)
- **Rhodes**: Usually closes early October (typically October 1-3)
- **Commonwealth**: Usually closes mid-December (typically December 15)

### US Scholarships
- **Fulbright**: Varies by country, typically April-May for most countries
- **Mastercard Foundation**: Varies by partner university, typically March-April

### European Scholarships
- **DAAD**: Typically April 30 for most programs
- **Erasmus Mundus**: Typically late February (February 28)
- **Swedish Institute**: Typically mid-February (February 15)
- **VLIR-UOS**: Typically early February (February 1)
- **Orange Knowledge Programme**: Typically mid-March (March 22)

### Other Regions
- **Australia Awards**: Typically April 30
- **Japan Africa Dream**: Typically April 30
- **Turkish Government**: Typically mid-February (February 20)
- **Chinese Government**: Typically April 30

## How to Verify Deadlines

1. Visit the official scholarship website (links are stored in the database)
2. Look for "Application Deadline", "Deadline", or "Apply By" sections
3. Note the exact date and time (if specified)
4. Update the database accordingly
5. Re-run the seed script after verification
